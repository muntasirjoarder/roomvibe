Very generic app idea. 
User is a regular user has a messy bathroom or office room that he would like to make stunning or better looking. 
He takes some photos from different angles. Uploads to the app. 
The app identifies from the photos the current status of the room. app can also identify and confirm what type of room it is and what are the issues that may be resolved.
User may or may not be able to provide the room planning or actual measurement. But from the analysis of the photos the app can get an idea of the dimension. app may also ask some small questions to get some more ideas.
App is trained with lots of stunning home renovation project images which it can use as inspiration. 
Now app can suggest some minor (e.g. add remove furnitures etc) or major (e.g. maybe put up a dry wall etc) changes of that premise to make it gorgeous and purposeful.
The app may not need to be super perfect with the final design but it needs to be logical. But as an output it will suggest few changes as bulle points as well as produce an after project image (how it may be when he does those changes). The user can then may be take it to some builders to do the actual measurement and project.
App should have bvery simple interface and easily understandable UX.
